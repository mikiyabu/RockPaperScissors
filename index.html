How to play

Rock paper scissors is a classic game thats been played for countless generations across the world thanks to it's ease if accessability and effectiveness at making decisions.

To play, click 'R', 'P', or 'S' to choose if you want to pick rock, paper, or scissors to play against the bot. Rock wins against scissors but loses to paper, paper wins against rock but loses to scissors, and scissors wins against rock but loses against paper.

In the case where if you and the bot choose the same hand, neither you nor the bot will recieve a point.

First to 3 points wins!
